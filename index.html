Sliding Input Privacy Policy & Support

Last Updated: June 23, 2026

This application (hereinafter referred to as "this App") is a completely offline, standalone application. We highly value your privacy and provide necessary technical support accordingly.

1. Data Collection and Usage

  • Completely Offline: This App does not require an internet connection to function and does not possess any network transmission capabilities.
  • Zero Data Collection: We do not collect, store, record, or upload any of your personal information, device data, geolocation, or behavioral data.
  • Local Processing: Text you enter in this App is processed only on your local device for display, editing, and candidate generation. It is not sent to the developer or any third party.
  • Clipboard: When you tap the copy button, the current text you entered is written to the system clipboard so you can paste it into other apps. This App does not read, record, upload, or share your clipboard content.
  • Local Storage Only: This App uses a local dictionary database to provide input candidates. Related data is saved only on your own local device. Uninstalling the App will remove the associated local data from the system.

2. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Since we do not collect or have access to any user data, it is absolutely impossible for us to share, rent, sell, or disclose your data to any third-party organizations or individuals.
